MagNA Bead Recipe:

0.1% carboxyl-modified Sera-Mag Magnetic Speed-beads (FisherSci, cat.#: 09-981-123) 
18% PEG-8000 (w/v) (e.g. Sigma Aldrich, cat.#: 89510) 
1M NaCl 
10mM Tris-HCl, ph 8.0 
1 mM EDTA, pH 8.0 
Optional: 0.05% Tween 20 

note: beads contain sodium azide, wash beads 2x with water or TE before addition.  
Store MagNA kit in the refrigerator in the dark and vigorously mix before use; kit is stable for at least 3 months.
